What our students say about Igniting Minds

IGM is the abode of learning that every student and parent would dream of. The dynamic activities that keep us students engaged regularly help us in transforming ourselves to be outgoing, self-aware and intellectually capable of understanding situations and adapting to prompts.

These include public speaking, writing, quizzing, debates and even other extra-curriculars like dance and sporting events too. Students from every walk of life are encouraged to take part. A worthwhile and astounding experience with various other higher-level English tests and opportunities in other competitions also provided!

English and its subsequent modes of communication are taken very seriously here and will definitely improve with leaps and bounds. It is the heart and soul of so many young minds and to all those who read these reviews - these aren't nearly sufficient to completely describe this institution.

Truly a marvellous community to whom I owe so much. This is one and truly the destination in this large city of ours which would undeniably help a growing student foster the necessary values for their future. Perfect for instilling overall development in the youth to become part of a well-faring society as a whole at the end of the day.

My heart goes out to all those who have ever guided me here. LONG LIVE IGNITING MINDS!!
