What We Offer

Our Programs

Comprehensive after-school activities designed for holistic development

Academic Support

We believe that true academic excellence is not defined by rote learning or exam marks alone, but by a student's ability to think critically, communicate confidently, and apply knowledge creatively.

We go beyond conventional tutoring by combining structured academic guidance with a focus on higher-order skills — under the mentorship of Cambridge-level teachers who bring global standards into every session.

A key feature is the integration of real-world issues into student-centric scenarios, stretching students' ability to think beyond routine, within-the-box thinking.

Creative Arts

We inculcate the foundations of the special and fine arts, establishing a milieu that can flourish artistically. Creativity and innovation is regarded as a pivotal form of expression that can shape the way a young student brings about transformation and meaningful impact.

Guided workshops, interdisciplinary projects and group performances are marvellous displays of how students immerse themselves in new dimensions of self-expression.

Cambridge Examinations

Our students are trained vigorously to take up the intermediate and advanced levels of Cambridge ESOL examinations — validating proficiency in reading, writing, speaking, and listening across the full spectrum of English language skills.

Our students routinely ace the exams, with most of them scoring distinction marks, reflecting the depth and rigour of our preparation approach.

Extra-Curricular Activities

We promote healthy competition through our four literary houses — Shakespeare, Wordsworth, Austen and Tagore — awarding points across all achievement fields.

Students participate in dance competitions, our cherished Sports Day, our historic Annual Day and our beloved cultural event IGNITE — spaces for out-of-the-box learning, personality-building, and heartfelt camaraderie.

World Scholars
Cup Program

Specialized training for academic excellence and global competition

Our specialized World Scholars Cup program prepares students to excel in this prestigious global academic competition with comprehensive training in all areas:

  • Collaborative Writing strategies and techniques
  • Scholar's Challenge subject mastery
  • Team Debate preparation and practice
  • Curriculum-aligned subject knowledge building
  • Mock tournaments and practice sessions
  • Critical thinking and rapid learning skills

Student Voices

What Our Students Say

Real stories from the Igniting Minds community

IGM is the abode of learning that every student and parent would dream of. The dynamic activities that keep us students engaged regularly help us in transforming ourselves to be outgoing, self-aware and intellectually capable.

These include public speaking, writing, quizzing, debates and even other extra-curriculars like dance and sporting events too. Students from every walk of life are encouraged to take part.

English and its subsequent modes of communication are taken very seriously here and will definitely improve with leaps and bounds. It is the heart and soul of so many young minds.

Truly a marvellous community to whom I owe so much. This is undeniably the destination in this large city which would help a growing student foster the necessary values for their future.

My heart goes out to all those who have ever guided me here. LONG LIVE IGNITING MINDS!!

Sachin Sathyasivan

Student at Igniting Minds

Forget boring tuitions — my real learning happens at Igniting Minds, and it's actually fun! Igniting Minds has made me so much more confident.

What I love is that they don't just focus on grades — they make sure students genuinely fall in love with learning. The teachers are experts who are passionate about teaching and help us deeply understand concepts instead of just making us memorize facts.

Igniting Minds constantly pushes us with big challenges, like training hard for the World Scholars Cup, which has taught me to think critically. The WSC preparation has massively boosted my speaking and debating skills, and the Cambridge exams have really improved my English fluency.

Igniting Minds doesn't just meet expectations — it has truly sparked a fire in several minds, including mine.

Kriya Jain

Student at Igniting Minds
