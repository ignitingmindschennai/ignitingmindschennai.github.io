About Us

Learn what makes Igniting Minds special

Our Mission

At Igniting Minds, we believe in the dictum, "Each child is special." Given the right exposure, at the right age, every child can hone their skills to their maximum potential. We commit to fostering a love for learning, celebrating curiosity, building confidence and creating a close-knit community of support and trust.

We make sure every child is seen, heard and valued, inspiring them to dream big and discover their strengths.

Our mission is to spark creativity and promote collaboration in an inclusive, joyful environment.

We integrate values into our learning, instilling integrity, caring, kindness, respect, empathy and responsibility, to ensure that our students are well rounded individuals with honesty, humility and compassion.

Academic Support

Academic Support

At this institution, we believe that true academic excellence is not defined by rote learning or exam marks alone, but by a student's ability to think critically, communicate confidently, and apply knowledge creatively. Our Academic Support is designed to nurture these very qualities — empowering every learner to reach their fullest potential.

We go beyond conventional tutoring by combining structured academic guidance with a focus on higher-order skills that prepare students for real-world success. Each session is thoughtfully crafted to help students build logical reasoning, enhance English fluency, and master the art of public speaking and presentation — under the mentorship of Cambridge-level teachers who bring global standards into every session.

A key feature in this regard is the intergration of real-world issues into student centric scenarios which stretch the students abiltiy to think beyond the confines of routine within the box thinking.

Cambridge Exams

Cambridge Examinations

In our pursuit of excellence, our students are trained vigorously to take up the intermediate and advanced levels of the Cambridge ESOL examinations. This is in tandem with many other dynamic and robust tests and assessments. These are done to validate a student's proficiency over the English language in the different disciplines of reading, writing, speaking and listening.

Our students routinely ace the exams, with most of them scoring distinction marks.

Extra-Curricular

Extra-Curricular Activities

To promote a sense of healthy competition between our own houses within the institution who are named after legends of the literary world – Shakespeare, Wordsworth, Austen and Tagore – points are awarded to all achievers regardless of any field in which they have made an accomplishment of value and honour.

On top of that, we allow our students to participate in a versatile set of extra-curricular events such as competitions in dance, our very own and much cherished Sports Day, our historic Annual Day and our beloved cultural event 'IGNITE'. We believe that these events can open up spaces for out-of-the-box learning, personality-building, heartfelt camaraderie and an equitable opportunity for all to compete against the best of the best to emerge victorious for their houses.

World Scholars Cup Preparation

Specialized training for academic excellence and global competition

Excel in Global Academic Competitions

Our specialized World Scholars Cup program prepares students to excel in this prestigious global academic competition. We provide comprehensive training in all areas of the competition:

  • Collaborative Writing strategies and techniques
  • Scholar's Challenge subject mastery
  • Team Debate preparation and practice
  • Curriculum-aligned subject knowledge building
  • Mock tournaments and practice sessions
  • Critical thinking and rapid learning skills

Our experienced training have a proven track record of helping students achieve top honors at regional and global rounds of the World Scholars Cup.
